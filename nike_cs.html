Learning

Testing in real environments was critical because it revealed the need for a light mode to handle glare on the training screen.

Context

SPARQ Sensory Performance is a system of technologies, products and programs designed to assess, analyze and improve an athlete's visual and sensory performance.

Problem / Opportunity

The Nike innovation Kitchen leadership was looking for a more inexpensive option to complete the design and development of the Sensory Training Station in a short amount of time with a vendor they could trust.

Contribution

I led the design and development of Nike’s SPARQ Sensory Performance Training OS and its companion mobile app, overseeing the entire process from concept to final production.

Impact

The system enabled trainers to administer standardized tests, generating actionable performance insights to improve athletes’ visual and motor skills. See it in action.

Applied UX Laws*

Hick's Law

The time it takes to make a decision increases with the number and complexity of choices.

Fitt's Law

The time to acquire a target is a function of the distance to and size of the target.
