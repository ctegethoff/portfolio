Learning

We learned that effective mobile UX isn’t about replicating the TV—it’s about designing complementary features that feel natural to the device and context.

Context

Roku was exploring how to expand the mobile app beyond just a remote—making it a hub for content discovery, app launching, and user generated sharing.

Problem / Opportunity

How might we transform mobile into a companion experience that encourages deeper engagement across devices and content types?

Contribution

Led early UX strategy for cross-device features like photo sharing, app launching, and content discovery.

Impact

Established foundational patterns that helped position Roku’s mobile app as a richer, more connected part of the overall ecosystem.

Applied UX Laws*

Mental Model

We form a working model in our minds around what we think we know about a system.

Peak-End Rule

People judge an experience largely based on how they felt at its peak and at its end, rather than the total sum or average of every moment of the experience.
