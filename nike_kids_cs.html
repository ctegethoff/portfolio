Learning

Using the diorama as a storytelling device tapped into a core part of Nike’s brand, creating an emotional connection that felt relatable for both parents and children.

Context

The Nike Innovation Kitchen needed a concept prototype for a digital destination that communicated Nike Kids’ product innovation, grounded in biometrics and child development research.

Problem / Opportunity

Create a shared digital space where parents and kids can explore the science behind Nike’s products together — making innovation relatable for adults and fun, engaging, and educational for children.

Contribution

Led the concept and design of an online experience that turned complex data into engaging storytelling around product benefits, making it fun and memorable.

Impact

Delivered a compelling prototype that laid groundwork for Nike’s future digital storytelling and community-building efforts for youth-focused products.

Applied UX Laws*

Flow

Make content and features available for discovery to avoid disengagement with the interface.

Aesthetic-Usability Effect

Users often perceive aesthetically pleasing designs as more usable.
