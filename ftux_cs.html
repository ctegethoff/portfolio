Learning

Pay attention to a wide spectrum of users, but you can learn a lot talking to the hard core gamers.

Context

Nearly 40% of users install Google Play Games, but never launch a game. 90% of users who do not install a game on day 1, become churned users.

Problem / Opportunity

The FTUX fails to communicate the platform’s unique value to new users.

Contribution

Create a share worthy FTUX for Google Play Games on PC that immediately showcases the platform’s unique value as a trusted brand, amplify the Play loyalty program, highlight superior performance on bigger screens, and make it effortless to find games to love. See my process

Impact

Projected 8-10% lift in day 1 game launches and projected lift in MAUs converting potential gamers into day one players.

Applied UX Laws*

Flow

Make content and features available for discovery to avoid disengagement with the interface.

Aesthetic-Usability Effect

Users often perceive aesthetically pleasing designs as more usable.
