Learning

Building a design system is only half the work. Without consistent onboarding, continued education, advocacy, and participation from the teams, design systems get dated really quickly.

Context

Roku needed a cohesive design system to unify visual language, reduce inconsistencies across platforms, and help define how the product could scale.

Problem / Opportunity

Design and engineering teams lacked a shared foundation, resulting in redundant work, inconsistent UI, and slower velocity. A Roku design system could streamline workflows and improve product quality.

Impact
