A significant portion of users go inactive shortly after installing Google Play Games for PC, creating a critical gap in early retention.

We formulated a multi-pronged strategy focused on reaching lapsed gamers, expanding developer impact, and ensuring notifications were genuinely useful to players. Each pillar was informed by real user behavior and needs.

Grounded in gamer and developer insights, we used Critical User Journeys (CUJs) to surface friction points and define where notifications could most effectively re-engage users and drive retention.

By delivering timely, relevant desktop notifications outside the app, we have a high-impact opportunity to re-engage lapsed gamers and drive long-term engagement.

Reconnecting lapsed gamers outside the app with a game they are already playing on mobile represents the biggest opportunity to reach lapsed gamers.
