Context

A significant portion of users went inactive shortly after installing Play Games for PC, exposing a critical gap in early retention.

Problem / Opportunity

Lapsed users weren't returning, and the app lacked meaningful ways to bring them back. We needed a way to re-engage them that felt relevant and timely, based on real gamer's needs.

Contribution

Grounded in gamer and developer insights, we used Critical User Journeys (CUJs) to surface friction points and define where notifications could most effectively re-engage users and drive retention.

Impact TBD

By delivering timely, relevant desktop notifications outside the app, we can create more opportunities to re-engage lapsed gamers and drive long-term engagement.

Applied UX Laws*

Choice Overload

The tendency for people to get overwhelmed when they are presented with a large number of options.

Goal Gradient Effect

The tendency to approach a goal increases with proximity to the goal.
