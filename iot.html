Learning

Iterating mid study revealed new opportunities and allowed us to explore unexpected directions, increasing the overall impact of the research.

Context

Roku was launching its first-to-market smart home experience, connecting IoT control across mobile and TV platforms for a unified user experience.

Problem / Opportunity

How might we design intuitive, cross-platform control for smart devices—balancing consistency with platform-specific interaction needs?

Contribution

Led early UX efforts, defining interaction models and design direction for Roku’s first smart home experience across mobile and TV.

Impact

Provided foundational design direction and early concepts that informed ongoing development of Roku's connected home experience.

Applied UX Laws*

Flow

Make content and features available for discovery to avoid disengagement with the interface.

Tesler's Law

The Law of Conservation of Complexity, states that for any system there is a certain amount of complexity which cannot be reduced.
