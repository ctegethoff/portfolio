Learning

Listening to customer support calls can be a powerful tool to build empathy and stay grounded in user needs.

Context

We suspected there were usability issues with Settings Over Video, but struggled where to start. To understand if there was a problem, we started collecting data by listening in on a lot of customer support calls.

Problem / Opportunity

Research highlighted Settings Over Video was a space where usability issues were firmly rooted in unclear hierarchy, ambiguous naming, and lack of user feedbackz

Contribution

Established a single source of truth for the new pattern and collaborated with engineering across development and QA to bring it to life. See my process

Impact

Improved usability and clarity in Settings Over Video, accelerated design-dev handoff, and established a scalable foundation for future cross-platform settings experiences.

Applied UX Laws*

Hick's Law

The time it takes to make a decision increases with the number and complexity of choices.

Choice Overload

The tendency for people to get overwhelmed when they are presented with a large number of options.
